Tactile Mapping and Localization

Maria Bauza, Oleguer Canal, Alberto Rodriguez

Summary [video, paper]

  • Reconstruction and localization using GelSlim
  • Accurate offline model generation, followed by online localization

Introduction

  • Motivation for GelSlim: High spatial resolution correlates to dexterity
  • Dense tactile descriptors: Can perform regression on dense signals
  • Crisp tactile memory: Data-association of tactile imprints
  • Contributions:
    • Local shape estimation: tactile imprint → CNN → local shape
    • Global tactile mapping: Combine tactile imprints with kinematics to get global shape
    • Object tactile localization: localize grasped object local shape to global model matching

Approach: Tactile mapping and localization

  • Comprises of three parts:
    1. local shape estimation
    1. global tactile and shape matching
    1. identification and localization

Local shape estimation

  • Training with objects of known heightmaps and tactile imprint images
    • 600 pairs of heightmaps and imprints
  • Standard CNN to train this mapping
    • Augment with random noise in 3 channels
    • Adding extra channels for translation x,yx, yx,y﻿ information
    • More information: website

Global tactile and shape mapping

  • Combines tactile imprints to get a global shape
  • Heightmap → local point cloud via calibration
  • Stitch all point clouds using the kinematic information
    • Do they not do alignment, naive method?

Tactile localization

  • Find correspondence between global tactile shape and local tactile imprints
  • Steps:
    1. Tactile imprint → heightmap → local shape (Section 4)
    1. Stitch local shape to global object
      • RANDOM: randomly selected pose
      • CTI: Quantify touch similarity via network and gripper size, use this pose
      • CTI-ICP-N: Get N closest images, stitch to get auxilary point cloud, and do ICP to align with that
        • If N = all imprints, it becomes ICP between local point cloud and global model
    1. Get object pose via kinematics

Discussion and future work

  • Combining visual information:
    • Practical system will incorporate vision to get better maps
    • Need a probabilistic way to combine sensing modalities (GPIS?)
  • Prior information:
    • Shape priors to bootstrap algorithms (GP 3D priors)

    • Lack of contact is also information (GP will take care of this)
  • Stitching:
